## Visual Direction
- Theme: light, premium, technical, product-first
- Background: warm mineral white, porcelain, brushed neutrals
- Text: deep graphite with softer slate support copy
- Accent: restrained electric blue with cool teal support
- Materials: frosted glass, matte cards, brushed metal dividers, restrained glows
- Type: premium grotesk for headings/body, mono for technical labels and UI metadata
- Layout: wide grid, anchored panels, fewer but stronger sections, screenshots carrying equal weight with copy

## Interaction Plan
- Hero: layered glass system object representing Websites, CRM, Automations, AI Agents, Analytics over one operating layer
- Workflow section: sticky explanatory panel with sequential operational cards
- Governance section: dark graphite reset showing approvals, audit history, and permissions beneath workflow
- Comparison section: fragmented stack vs unified operating layer
- Motion style: quiet, mechanical, precise; no decorative floating gimmicks

## Copy Principles
- Hero headline: 6-10 words
- Hero subhead: concise, operational, buyer-facing
- One question per section
- Product language over consultancy language
- No placeholder proof counters
- Use realistic system states, not vague “AI magic” claims
