# Ingenium Consulting — Website Outline

> Full website structure and page-level outline for a modern SaaS website that emphasizes website + CRM + marketing automation + AI.

## 1. Homepage

### Hero
- Headline: `Launch a connected website that works with your CRM, marketing tools, and AI.`
- Subhead: `Ingenium Consulting builds websites that do more than launch — they become the center of your digital growth engine.`
- Primary CTA: `Book a demo`
- Secondary CTA: `See how it works`
- Supporting line: `Designed for startups and SMEs that want fast, reliable growth without disconnected systems.`
- Visual: split-screen with product UI preview/dashboard and connection diagram, or clean illustration showing Website → CRM → AI.

### Why Ingenium
- Short intro explaining the integrated system: `Website + CRM + Marketing + AI all working together.`
- Three benefits cards:
  1. `Connected data` — `Every lead, campaign, and customer interaction updates in one place.`
  2. `AI-assisted workflows` — `Smart agents act on your business data, so you move faster.`
  3. `Built for growth` — `Launch quickly, optimize continuously, and scale with confidence.`

### How it Works
- Section title: `How Ingenium works`
- Four-step system flow:
  1. `Website captures leads` — smart forms, chat, landing pages.
  2. `CRM stores every interaction` — custom-built system tuned to your sales and marketing.
  3. `Marketing activates automatically` — email, SMS, campaign journeys.
  4. `AI agents act on your data` — follow-up, insights, optimization.
- Visual: horizontal cards with icons and connecting lines.

### Features
- Section title: `Core capabilities`
- Feature list with short benefits:
  - `CRM-connected website`: `Your site and pipeline talk to each other in real time.`
  - `Automated campaigns`: `Launch follow-up flows without manual setup.`
  - `Lead intelligence`: `Score and route leads automatically.`
  - `AI-driven insights`: `See recommendations, next steps, and growth opportunities.`
  - `Custom reporting`: `Measure what matters and keep teams aligned.`

### Audience Blocks
- Section title: `Built for startups and SMEs`
- Two audience panels:
  - `Startups`: `Launch faster with a connected digital platform that supports growth from day one.`
  - `SMEs`: `Replace fragmented tools with a system that keeps your marketing and sales coordinated.`
- Optional third panel: `Marketing teams`: `Keep campaigns, funnels, and data in sync with one partner.`

### Proof & Trust
- Testimonials or result highlights.
- Example statement: `“We cut lead response time in half and now run campaigns from one platform.”`
- Small logos or outcome stats: `3x faster follow-up`, `20% higher conversion`, `1 system instead of 4.`

### Product Snapshot
- Section title: `See the platform in action`
- Image of dashboard or interface screenshot.
- Key metrics or labels: `Leads`, `Campaigns`, `AI tasks`, `Conversion rate`.
- Supporting copy: `A single view of every contact, website lead, and campaign result.`

### Call to Action
- Reinforce with a final CTA block.
- Headline: `Ready to build a smarter website system?`
- CTA buttons: `Start your demo` / `Talk to an expert`

## 2. Services Page

### Page purpose
Explain what Ingenium delivers, how the process works, and why clients choose this connected approach.

### Sections
1. `What we build`
   - `Custom website development` — `High-performance, conversion-focused sites built for your brand.`
   - `CRM integration` — `A custom CRM that stores, scores, and routes leads automatically.`
   - `Marketing automation` — `Email, nurture, and campaign automation that runs from your data.`
   - `AI-enabled workflows` — `Agents that help manage campaigns, follow-up, and reporting.`

2. `The process`
   - Discovery: `Understand your business, audience, and growth goals.`
   - Build: `Design and develop your connected site and CRM experience.`
   - Connect: `Integrate website capture, CRM records, and marketing journeys.`
   - Optimize: `Review performance, refine copy, and improve outcomes.`

3. `Why this matters`
   - `No more data silos`.
   - `Faster lead response with automation.`
   - `A single source of truth for growth activity.`
   - `Smarter decisions powered by AI and insights.`

4. `Service packages` (optional)
   - `Launch` — `Ready-to-go site + CRM setup for new businesses.`
   - `Growth` — `Ongoing optimization, automation, and campaign support.`
   - `Enterprise` — `Custom workflows, data integrations, and AI assistants.`

5. `CTA`
   - `Book a discovery call`
   - `Get a tailored scope`

## 7. Optional Additional Pages

### Case Study Page
- Problem statement, solution, results.
- Focus on `connected website + CRM + automation` outcomes.
- Metrics like `lead response time`, `campaign performance`, `growth impact.`

### Resources / Blog Page
- Short articles on website optimization, CRM best practices, AI automation.
- Examples: `How to connect your website to CRM`, `When AI should run your campaigns`, `How startups can build better lead follow-up.`

### Pricing or Packages Page
- If pricing is public, use simple tier cards.
- Emphasize `what’s included` and `why connected systems are worth it`.
- Use contrast between package benefits rather than budget pricing.

## 8. Content and Copy Notes

- Keep copy outcome-focused, not feature-heavy.
- Use friendly but professional language.
- Reinforce the integrated platform value on every page.
- Use microcopy to explain acronyms or technical terms.
- Add small value statements under each CTA.

## 9. Navigation and UX

- Sticky top navigation with `Services`, `Platform`, `About`, `Contact`.
- Use section anchors for long pages: `How it works`, `Features`, `Why us`.
- Keep page lengths comfortable with clear sections and whitespace.
- Prioritize mobile-first readability.
