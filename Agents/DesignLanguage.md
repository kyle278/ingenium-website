# Ingenium Website Design Language

- Version: 3.0
- Scope: public marketing site
- Goal: keep the site light, premium, technical, and product-first

## Product Aesthetic
- Warm mineral-white canvas
- Deep graphite typography
- Restrained electric blue and cool teal accents
- Frosted glass, brushed metal, matte surfaces
- Quiet enterprise confidence without corporate bloat

## Typography
- Display: `Manrope` via `--font-display`
- Body: `Public Sans` via `--font-body`
- Mono: `Azeret Mono` via `--font-mono`

## Core Rules
- Lead with product architecture, not consultancy framing
- Use screenshots, portal panels, and system diagrams early
- Keep copy concise and buyer-facing
- Avoid placeholder metrics and empty proof counters
- Use dark sections sparingly as pacing resets only

## Surface System
- Light card baseline: `mineral-panel`
- Dark reset card: `graphite-panel`
- Technical tags: `tech-pill`
- Motion: `cta-lift`, `panel-hover`, `timeline-step`, `data-flow-dot`

## Layout
- Max width: `1280px`
- Default rhythm: `space-y-24 md:space-y-32`
- Wide split heroes and anchored supporting panels
- Mobile-first responsive layouts; no desktop-only storytelling

## CTAs
- Primary: `Book Demo`
- Secondary: `See the Platform`
- Tertiary: `Get a Revenue Systems Teardown`

## Do Not
- Revert to dark cyber SaaS styling
- Use purple-heavy AI visuals
- Depend on long paragraphs
- Treat proof as anonymous counters
